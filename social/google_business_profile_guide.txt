Infinium Services / MedEco Cleaning

Follow these steps to set up your Google Business Profile so customers can find you on Google Search and Google Maps.

---

STEP 1: GO TO GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE
--------------------------------------
1. Open your browser and go to: https://business.google.com
2. Sign in with a Google account (Gmail). Use one tied to your business if you have one.
3. Click "Manage now" or "Add your business to Google"

STEP 2: ENTER YOUR BUSINESS NAME
---------------------------------
• Business name: Infinium Services
• If Google suggests existing businesses, select yours if it appears.
  Otherwise click "Add your business to Google"

STEP 3: CHOOSE YOUR BUSINESS CATEGORY
--------------------------------------
• Primary category: "House Cleaning Service"
• Additional categories you can add later:
  - "Commercial Cleaning Service"
  - "Janitorial Service"
  - "Office Cleaning Service"

STEP 4: ADD YOUR LOCATION
--------------------------
• If you have a physical office/storefront: Enter the address
• If you travel to customers (service area business):
  - Select "I deliver goods and services to my customers"
  - Enter your service areas:
    → Toronto
    → Mississauga
    → Brampton
    → Vaughan
    → Markham
    → Richmond Hill
    → Pickering
    → Ajax
    → Oshawa
    → Oakville
    → Burlington
    → Milton

STEP 5: ADD CONTACT INFORMATION
--------------------------------
• Phone: (416) 726-5507
• Website: https://www.infiniumservices.ca

STEP 6: VERIFY YOUR BUSINESS
------------------------------
Google will need to verify you own the business. Options:
• Phone verification (fastest — they call or text a code)
• Postcard verification (Google mails a postcard with a PIN, takes 5-14 days)
• Video verification (record a short video of your business)

Choose phone verification if available — it's the quickest.

---------------------------------------------------
Once verified, fill in EVERYTHING:

A) BUSINESS DESCRIPTION (copy this):

Our cleaning protocols are designed by medical professionals, featuring colour-coded microfibre systems, hospital-grade disinfection, and cross-contamination prevention. We use 100% eco-friendly products to deliver a medical-standard clean that's safe for your family and pets. Serving Toronto, Peel, York, Durham, and Halton regions.

D) SERVICES — Add these:

E) ATTRIBUTES:

STEP 8: GET REVIEWS
---------------------
After every job, ask happy customers to leave a Google review.
• Share your review link (Google provides this in your dashboard)
• You can also text it to customers after service

STEP 9: POST REGULARLY
-----------------------
Use Google Business posts to share:
• Special offers & promotions
• Before/after photos
• The social media content I created for you
• Seasonal cleaning tips

Post at least once a week for best results.

---

TIPS FOR RANKING HIGHER ON GOOGLE:
===================================
1. Complete 100% of your profile — every field matters
2. Get reviews — aim for 5+ reviews in the first month
3. Post weekly updates with photos
4. Respond to all reviews (even negative ones — be professional)
5. Keep hours and info accurate
6. Add new photos monthly
7. Your website SEO is already done — Google will pick up the structured data
