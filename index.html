Why Choose MedEco

Not All Cleaning Is Created Equal

Designed by Medical Professionals

MedEco was designed by medical professionals who understand that standard cleaning methods can actually spread germs rather than eliminate them.

How Standard Cleaning Can Spread Germs

Most conventional cleaning services use the same cloths across multiple rooms, transferring bacteria from bathrooms to kitchens and living areas.

Standard vacuum cleaners lack adequate filtration, recirculating fine dust and allergens back into your air.

Without proper protocols, incorrect chemical usage can leave surfaces inadequately sanitised — or worse, create harmful residues.

Designed by Medical Professionals

MedEco is designed and managed by medical professionals with infection-control expertise. We apply hospital-level hygiene awareness to residential cleaning.

Every member of our team is trained in cross-contamination prevention — bathroom tools never touch kitchen surfaces.

We use only eco-friendly, Health Canada-approved disinfectants that are bleach-free, low-fume, and safe for children, pets, seniors, and allergy sufferers.
